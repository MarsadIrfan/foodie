Delicious Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
2. Heat marinara sauce in a pan.
3. Mix the cooked pasta with the sauce.
4. Sprinkle cheese and serve hot.
