Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, corn)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1-2 green onions (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
2. Add the beaten eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
3. In the same pan, saut√© onions and mixed vegetables until soft.
4. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
5. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix well.
6. Garnish with green onions, and season with salt and pepper.
7. Serve hot as a side dish or main course.
