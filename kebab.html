Traditional Kebab

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced beef or lamb
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 egg (optional, to bind)
  • 2 tbsp oil for frying or grilling

Instructions

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the minced meat, chopped onion, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and all the spices.
2. Add fresh coriander leaves and salt. Mix well to combine.
3. Optionally, add an egg to help bind the mixture together.
4. Shape the mixture into round or oval patties.
5. Heat oil in a frying pan or on a grill. Fry or grill the kebabs until golden brown on both sides (about 5-7 minutes per side).
6. Serve hot with naan, rice, or a side of salad, and enjoy with mint chutney or yogurt.
