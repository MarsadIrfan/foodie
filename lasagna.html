Classic Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 500 g ricotta cheese
  • 300 g mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (for garnish)

Instructions

1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
5. Layer the lasagna: start with a thin layer of sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella.
6. Repeat the layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top.
7. Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
8. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with Parmesan cheese.
