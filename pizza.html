Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 200 g pizza sauce
  • 200 g mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 100 g pepperoni slices
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Oregano (for garnish)

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (428°F).
2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
4. Top with shredded mozzarella, pepperoni, onion, and bell pepper.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano on top.
6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
7. Slice and serve hot.
