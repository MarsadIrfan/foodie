Traditional Beef Pulao

Ingredients

  • 1 kg basmati rice
  • 500 g beef (cut into cubes)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • Whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 2 cups yogurt
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
2. Heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown.
3. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomatoes, and whole spices. Cook until tomatoes soften.
4. Add beef and cook until browned.
5. Add yogurt, salt, and water. Let it simmer until the beef is tender.
6. Add soaked rice and cook in a pressure cooker or on low heat.
7. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves.
8. Serve hot with raita or salad.
