Traditional Seekh Kebab

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced beef or chicken
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 egg (optional, to bind)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil for grilling

Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, combine minced meat, chopped onion, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and all the spices.
2. Add fresh coriander and mint leaves. Mix well and knead the mixture for 5-7 minutes.
3. Optionally, add an egg to bind the mixture and ensure it holds together during grilling.
4. Shape the mixture onto skewers, pressing it tightly to form long kebabs.
5. Heat a grill or pan and brush with oil. Grill the kebabs on medium heat, turning occasionally until they are golden brown and cooked through (about 10-15 minutes).
6. Serve hot with naan or rice, and a side of mint chutney or salad.
